Biography

Arthur Nery is a Filipino singer-songwriter from Cagayan de Oro. He quickly rose to fame in the OPM scene thanks to his distinctive falsetto and emotional songwriting. He is known for his smooth vocals, falsettos and hit songs that resonate with the brokenhearted. (Good for yearning, proved and tested.)

Top Songs

  • Pagsamo: A heartbreaking ballad that went viral for its smooth vocals and sad lyrics.
  • Higa: A chill, lo-fi influenced track and a vibe perfect for late nights.
  • Binhi: A soulfoul song about growing love that helped establish his signature sound. One of his earlier hits.
  • TAKE ALL THE LOVE: A yearning song. Yearning for someone who's yearning someone else.
