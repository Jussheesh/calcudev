Biography

Alexander O'Connor known professionally as Rex Orange County, is a British Artist who mixes Indie Pop, Jazz, and Hip-Hop. He started out making "bedroom pop" and gained massive attention after collaborating with Tyler the Creator. His music is known for sounding bright and upbeat musically, even when the lyrics are actually quite self-deprecating or emotional.
