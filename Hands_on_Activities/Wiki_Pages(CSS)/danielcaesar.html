Biography

Daniel Caesar is a Canadian singer-songwriter who brought gospel influences back into mainstream R&B. His debut album, Freudian, was a critical success, earning him a Grammy. He is known for his stripped-back acoustic guitar ballads and his ability to create an intimate atmosphere in his music.

Top Songs

  • Best Part (feat. H.E.R.): A modern wedding staple and one of the most streamed R&B songs of the decade.
  • Get You (feat. Kali Uchis): A sultry, bass-heavy track that put him on the map.
  • Japanese Denim: A solo track showcasing his lyrical storytelling.
