Biography

Bruno Mars is an American-Filipino singer, songwriter, and producer known for his retro showmanship. He can do it all—sing, dance, and play multiple instruments. His style ranges from 80s funk and disco to 90s R&B. He is famous for his high-energy performances and his band, The Hooligans.

Top Songs

  • 24K Magic: A funk-disco anthem that screams luxury and partying.
  • Talking to the Moon: A piano ballad that showcases his softer side.
  • Locked Out of Heaven: A high-energy rock/pop track influenced by The Police.
