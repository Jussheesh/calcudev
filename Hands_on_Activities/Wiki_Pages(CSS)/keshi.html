Biography

Casey Luong, known as Keshi, was arguably the king of the "SoundCloud Lo-Fi" era before breaking into the mainstream. A former nurse, he started making beats in his bedroom. His sound is characterized by distant, falsetto vocals, acoustic guitar loops, and trap-influenced drums. He sings often about loneliness, distance, and complicated relationships.

Top Songs
